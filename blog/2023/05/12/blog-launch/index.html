Site Launch

We are terribly pleased with ourselves as we announce the launch of this site! It's almost embarassing. It makes us feel like going out for a long lunch that includes a little day drinking. After all, moments like this don't come along very often.

I'd like to thank everyone here at our imaginary company for their hard work and dedication. People have sacrificed almost an hour (or so) of their personal lives for this, so I hope you appreciate it.

I'd also like to thank the people who built the Sculpin static site generator. Without them, we wouldn't have been able to stand this thing up in such a short time, and with so little effort.

Now that you've found us, don't be a stranger. Check back for strange, new content from our imaginary team of talented writers. And remember that all of your dreams are only a blog away.

With kind regards,

The Editor
